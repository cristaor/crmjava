hipergate privacy statement

KnowGate is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy statement applies to the data collected by KnowGate through hipergate.

Collection of Your Information

We will ask you when we need information that personally identifies you or allows us to contact you. The personal information we collect may include your name, e-mail address, and other information that you upload to the services offered through hipergate. We may collect information about your visit, including the hipergate features that you use, and other actions you take in connection with hipergate. We also may collect certain standard computer information that your browser sends to every web site you visit, such as your IP address, browser type and language, and access times.

In order to offer you a more consistent and personalized experience in your interactions with KnowGate, information collected through hipergate may be combined with information obtained through other KnowGate services. We may also supplement the information we collect with information obtained from other companies. For example, we may use services from other companies that enable us to derive a general geographic area based on your IP address in order to customize certain services to your geographic area.

Use of Your Information

The information collected during hipergate registration process will be used to provide you with more effective customer service, improve the product and any other related KnowGate products or services, perform statistical analysis.

We may use your information to provide you with important information about the product or service that you are using, including critical updates and notifications. Additionally, with your permission, we may send you information about other KnowGate products and services, and/or share your personal information with KnowGate partners so they may send you information about their products and services.

KnowGate occasionally hires other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as web site hosting, packaging, mailing, and delivering prizes, answering customer questions about products and services, and sending information about our products, special offers, and other new services. We will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of the information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.

We may access or disclose information about you, including the content of your communications, in order to: (a) comply with the law or respond to lawful requests or legal process; (b) protect the rights or property of KnowGate or our customers, including the enforcement of our agreements or policies governing your use of the services; or (c) act on a good faith belief that such access or disclosure is necessary to protect the personal safety of customers or the public.

Information that is collected on hipergate may be stored and processed in Spain or any other country in which KnowGate or its affiliates, subsidiaries, or service providers maintain facilities.

Control of Your Information

Except as otherwise described in this statement, personal information you provide on hipergate will not be shared outside of KnowGate and its controlled subsidiaries and affiliates without your permission.

You can stop the delivery of future promotional e-mail from KnowGate sites and services by following the specific instructions in the e-mail you receive.

Please be aware that this privacy statement and any choices you make on hipergate will not necessarily apply to personal information you may have provided to KnowGate in the context of other, separately operated, KnowGate products or services.

Access to Your Personal Information

You may view or edit your personal information online at http://www.hipergate.org. In order to help prevent your personal information from being viewed by others, you will be required to sign in with your credentials (e-mail address and password). Alternatively, you can e-mail us at info@hipergate.org and we will contact you within 30 days regarding your request.

Security of Your Information

At KnowGate, we take the security of your personal information very seriously. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer servers with limited access that are located in controlled facilities.

Contact Information

KnowGate welcomes your comments regarding this privacy statement. If you have questions about this statement or believe that we have not adhered to it, please contact us via e-mail at info@hipergate.org.
